Terms and Policies to Remember

Guidelines

Introduction

The guidelines to be followed by the students are mentioned below.

First section

We are not responsible for the content or any viruses etc. on sites that we may link to.

Second section

We comply with all relevant laws on Privacy and Data Protection. In general, this means that we will only collect or process personal information for specific and lawful purposes, we won’t collect more than we need for those purposes or keep it for longer than necessary, we’ll do our best to keep it accurate, and we’ll keep it as safe as we can.

Third section

You need to match following requirements before you enroll to any of the courses.

If you disagree with someone's ideas you will discuss their ideas, rather than criticize or attack them personally.

Fourth section

Students are not allowed to share their login information with anyone

If a student tries to login in an account but fails after multiple attempts, then the account gets locked.

Fifth section

Your activities on an Online Course are shared with the course provider for academic research purposes. This includes the comments you make where you may disclose certain personal information about yourself.

Sixth section

Students should be well aware about the plagiarism they might get if they try to cheat or copy from any of their fellow friends for the assignments.

Seventh section

You are supportive and constructive when offering feedback to other learners in activities such as Peer Review and discussions.

Eighth section

You are here to learn, not to advertise products or services, and you will not use Acad-E-mics as a platform for campaigning.
